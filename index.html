Hi, I'm Maria Sofia Bucarelli

I'm a CNRS postdoctoral researcher in the COATI team, under the supervision of 3IA chair Emanuele Natale. COATI is a joint team involving Inria, CNRS, and Université Côte d'Azur. My research spans the theory and applications of machine learning, including representation learning, graph neural networks, learning from noisy labels, and model merging. I completed my PhD in Data Science in 2024 at Sapienza University of Rome, working with Prof. Fabrizio Silvestri and the RSTless group.
