Hi, I'm Maria Sofia Bucarelli

I'm a PhD student at the Department of Computer, Control and Management Engineering at Sapienza University of Rome, working under Prof. Fabrizio Silvestri and the RSTless group. My research focuses on the theory and applications of machine learning, including generalization bounds, learning with noisy labels, and neural network interpretability.

Research interests

Area 01

Machine Learning

Theory and applications of ML, including learning with noisy labels, clustering, and neural network interpretability.

Area 02

Learning Theory

Generalization bounds and theoretical foundations for modern machine learning algorithms and models.

Area 03

Foundations of ML

Mathematical underpinnings of learning algorithms, including projective clustering and inter-rater agreement.
